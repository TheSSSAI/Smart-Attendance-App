Molecular Component: Attendance List Item

Displays a single attendance record. The component has several variants to adapt to different contexts, such as the user's role and whether multi-selection is active.

Component Variations

Variant Name: "subordinate_view"

This is the default view for a Subordinate looking at their own attendance history. The date is the most prominent piece of information.
